Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

GigTracker is a personal earnings tracker for gig workers. We built it to be useful, not to collect your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and what we never do.

01 What we collect

Your name

Stored locally on your device only. Used to personalize the dashboard greeting. Never transmitted to our servers.

Shift & expense data

Earnings, hours, mileage, and expenses you log manually. Synced to a secure cloud database (Supabase) so your data is backed up and available across devices.

Location data

Used only during active GPS tracking to calculate mileage for a shift. Location is not stored continuously, not shared with third parties, and not used for advertising.

AI assistant messages

When you use the AI assistant, your questions and relevant earnings context are sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a response. These are not stored by us beyond your current session.

Purchase information

Subscription status is managed by RevenueCat. We do not store or have access to your payment details — those are handled entirely by Apple.

02 What we never do

  • Sell your data to third parties Never
  • Use your data for advertising Never
  • Share your earnings data with your gig platforms Never
  • Track your location in the background Never
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other apps Never

03 Third-party services

GigTracker uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Supabase

Cloud database for syncing your shift and expense data. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. supabase.com/privacy

RevenueCat

Manages subscription status and purchase verification. revenuecat.com/privacy

Anthropic (Claude API)

Powers the AI assistant feature. Your messages are processed per Anthropic's usage policies. anthropic.com/privacy

04 Data retention & deletion

Your shift and expense data is stored as long as you have an account. You can delete all data at any time from Settings → Clear All Data inside the app. This permanently removes your data from our servers.

05 Children's privacy

GigTracker is intended for adults (18+) who work on gig platforms. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.

06 Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
